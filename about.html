About Us

The T List Adventure Company is dedicated to taking family adventures all over the globe. From the beaches of Bali to the islands of the Galapagos, we are dedicated to exploration and learning. Come travel with us and experience the adventure!

the gang

Motivated by a joint love of traveling, we created a family determined to go everywhere and see everything. A lot's been done but we are nowhere close to being finished!

Tolby

Tolby has been a T List Adventure guide since 2010. Currently in the third grade, he travels beyond what his rigorous academic schedule allows. His travels have taken him all over North America to South America and Asia. His favorite place in the world is the Galapagos.
