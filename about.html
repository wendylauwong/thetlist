About Us

The T List Adventure Company is dedicated to taking family adventures all over the globe. From the beaches of Bali to the islands of the Galapagos, we are dedicated to exploration and learning. Come travel with us and experience the adventure!

the gang

Motivated by our joint love of traveling, we created a family determined to go everywhere and see everything. We've done a lot but in no way are we close to being done. We believe in packing light and pretty much only take home pictures as souvenirs. We have said yes to the occasional local market trinket.

Tolby

Tolby has been a T List Adventure guide since 2010. Currently in the third grade, he travels beyond what his rigorous academic schedule allows. His travels have taken him all over North America to South America and Asia. His favorite place in the world is the Galapagos.

Kian

Kian brings fresh eyes to our travels at the T List Adventure Company. Kian joined in June of 2015 and since then, he's been non-stop planning for the next plane ride. He believes that the journey is more important than the destination. It is his goal to travel by space shuttle one day.
